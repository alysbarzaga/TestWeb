Welcome to our Website

Basic Machinery and Acoustics Corporation is the country’s leading supplier and installer of various types of Acoustic Treatments. Acoustic treatments are essential to reducing excessive reverberation, reducing sound transmission and vibration control.

IN LAYMAN’S TERMS, acoustic treatments tune’s a room, minimizes noise, and makes the sound quality within the room more pleasant to the ears.

As a full-service Specialty Contractor, Manufacturer and supplier we have been serving the Architectural, Commercial and Industrial markets for over 21 years and counting, combining experience with expertise in sound isolation acoustical treatment and noise / vibration control.

As one of the leaders in the industry, we offer complete turnkey supply and installation packages for many types of Acoustic treatments. We conduct complete and certified Noise Surveys and estimates of Acoustic Treatment, submit CAD drawings of the design, and coordinate with clients’ Architects, Contractors, and Project Designers of choice.
